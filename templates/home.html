Bringing the Cutting Edge to Anchorage

Hi, I'm Paul Oliva, founder of Alaska Tech Consulting. I've been making websites since I was a kid, when I created a simple page to post professional wrestling results for my freinds (the internet filters at my middle school blocked the other sites.) I studied computer systems engineering at UAA, and then spent a year in Silicon Valley working for a data analytics start up. The pay was good and I learned a lot, but I eventually found myself missing Alaska's beautiful landscapes and great people.

When I got back to Anchorage, I noticed that most IT firms here aren't operating at maximum efficiency; in addition to using outdated, inefficient technology, their brick-and-mortar approach to IT creates large overheard costs that they pass on to you, the consumer. My philosophy in business is to use the most up-to-date tech to get the job done right, and quickly, the first time. I don't have a large office or administrative staff to pay, so I can charge you less than the other guys and still make a buck.
